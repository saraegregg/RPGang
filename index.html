Hi! We are RPGang Podcast

We tell stories and play DnD for fun!

And maybe for therapy?

We are a bit of a rag-tag gang of midwesterners who tell our stories through oddball characters exploring all kinds of settings.

Currently we are mid-campaing in a Victorian Gothic--everybody has a deep, dark secret to hide at all costs, as well as BIG problems to solve: How do we escape all these ghosts? Can I just shoot them? Why are they haunting us? How do I get home?
